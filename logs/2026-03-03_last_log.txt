AI-generated summary Knowledge-based visual question answering (KB-VQA) demonstrates significant potential for handling knowledge-intensive tasks. Current knowledge conflict mitigation methods primarily adapted from language-based approaches, focusing on context-level conflicts through engineered prompting strategies or context-aware decoding mechanisms.
